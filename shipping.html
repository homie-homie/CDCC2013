Shipping & Return Policy

Shipping Information

We ship nationwide via trusted courier services. All orders are processed within 1–3 business days. Delivery typically takes 3–7 business days depending on your location.

Shipping rates are calculated at checkout. You will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number once your order has shipped.

Return & Refund Policy

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you may return your item(s) within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging.

To initiate a return, please contact us at brand@service.com.my with your order number and reason for return.

Refunds will be issued to your original payment method within 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item(s).

Non-Returnable Items

Sale items, gift cards, and custom orders are non-returnable and non-refundable.

Questions?

For any questions, reach out to us via email or our social media platforms. We're happy to help!
